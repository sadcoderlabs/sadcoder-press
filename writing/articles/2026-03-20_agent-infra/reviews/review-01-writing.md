## Summary

- **Status:** Issues Found

- **Rules violated:** Not A but rather B, Summary sentences at paragraph end
- **Overview:** The article is well-sourced and specific throughout, with a clear framework and good authorial voice overall. Three mechanical violations were found and fixed: two instances of the "not A, but rather B" contrast formula and one summary sentence at paragraph end.

## Changes

### 1. Removed "not A, but rather B" contrast in opening pivot

- **Location:** Opening section, closing paragraph
- **Rule:** Not A, but rather B (overused contrast formula)
- **Original:** > 問題不再是「agent 需不需要自己的基礎建設」，而是：需要哪些？哪些最重要？目前有誰在做？
- **Revised:** > agent 需要自己的基礎建設，這件事已經清楚了。還沒清楚的是：需要哪些？哪些最重要？目前有誰在做？
- **Reason:** "不再是...而是" is a direct Chinese equivalent of "not A, but rather B." Rephrased as two separate sentences.

### 2. Removed "not A, but rather B" contrast in Visa/Mastercard description

- **Location:** 底層：身份與 Compute — Visa/Mastercard paragraph
- **Rule:** Not A, but rather B (overused contrast formula)
- **Original:** > 的核心不是封鎖 bot，而是識別合法的 agent。
- **Revised:** > 的切入點是識別合法 agent。
- **Reason:** "核心不是...而是..." is the same formula. Revised phrasing is more direct.

### 3. Replaced summary sentence at paragraph end with a specific observation

- **Location:** 底層：身份與 Compute — identity paragraph
- **Rule:** Summary sentences at paragraph end
- **Original:** > 但這些都還是早期方案，離「標準」很遠。
